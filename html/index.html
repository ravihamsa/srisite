WHAT I BELIEVE

According to me Interactive design is not just an art though it has to be aesthetic but it is fundamentally functional. Every design process is primarily driven by the purpose of the respective product / application's goal. However, design is also not a simple engineering process. Branding, color & usability often work at more 'abstract' levels and their effective development depends on many subtle factors. I try and maintain a clear philosophy when I generate a Visual design for web applications and animation based project elegance through simplicity, brevity without sacrificing functionality.

PHILOSOPHY

There are a few guiding principles that strongly influence my creative work. I strive for simplicity of values and functionality through creativity and aesthetic success. I push for truthfulness, personality and substance in design, to bring visual value. I see design as a tool to contribute as part of the ecosystem for the betterment of surrounding in which I live and share.

BACKGROUND

I was born and raised in Shimoga a small town in South India. I have studied Visual Communication and Multimedia design, and received a Bachelor of Fine Arts from Ken School Of Arts Bangalore, India.
